A tribute to Linus Torvalds

Mouse over a picture to see facts about Linus!

Who is Linus?

Linus Torvalds is the creator and principle developer of the Linux Kernal.

What is Linux?

Linux is an operating system, one of the most widely used ones on the planet!
It is the supremely stable and efficient operating system that runs much of the Internet.

Personal Life

Linus was born in Helsinki, Finland on December 28, 1969.
He was named after the American chemist: Linus Pauling.

Sparking Interest

He began his career programming BASIC on the family computer, a Commodore VIC-20.
He went on to get his master's in Computer Science from the University of Helsinki.

Linux Released

1991 saw the first snapshots of linux being released, with version 1.0 arriving in 1994.
Torvalds said he created it because he didn't have enough money for a UNIX system.

Did you know...

Linus also created the widely popular version control system Git

Still Kicking!

Torvalds continues to work on the Linux Kernal to this very day.
